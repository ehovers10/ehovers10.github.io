about me

I am a graduate student in the philosophy department at Rutgers University. Before making my way out to Jersey, I received my bachelor's and master's degrees in philosophy from the University of Wyoming.

about this site

On this site, you will (soon) find links to some of my philosophical work, which is focused on investigating formal models of the influence of context on natural language interpretation. In addition, if you are looking for information for one of my classes, you can access resources relevant to the course. I've also started blogging a little bit. Right now, the output is mostly notes on or small snippets from my dissertation.
